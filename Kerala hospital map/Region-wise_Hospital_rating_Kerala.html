Regional Hospital Rating

Markers in this interactive map represents the number of hospitals in each region. When zooming in, the areas are separated into sub-regions until individual markers identified. While clicking on a specific marker, it will display the name of the hospital and its public rating out of five. The heatmap displays average rating of all hospitals within each region. A higher grade is indicated by a color code ranging from blue to red.
*Findings are given below the map

Findings

1. Considering both the number of hospitals and their rating, the region from Kochi to Irinjalakuda exhibits best result.
2. There are significantly less hospitals in northern Kerala (Malabar area).
3. There is a considerable lack of hospitals between Kozhikode and Kannur, as well as in the hilly areas of south Kerala.
4. Hospitals in Malappuram district have highest public rating.
5. The number of hospitals and their public rating are reduced as moving away from coastal regions.
6. Despite the fact that Trivandrum has the most hospitals (23) after Kochi (30), the average public rating is lower.

Disclaimers

1. The list of hospitals is obtained from https://www.medicineindia.org/hospitals-in-state/kerala. The data is unofficial and unverfied.
2. Hospital rating is collected from Google Maps on Nov 2021. Hospitals with no rating or a rating with less number of voting are discarded.
3. Locations obtained using geocode function is not verified.
4. The results are unofficial and not verified.
